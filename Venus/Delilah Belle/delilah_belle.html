The Inspiration

Rocker-glam meets California-cool in The Monarch collection, inspired by Delilah Belle’s love of butterflies. Find odes to the transformative winged beauty in unexpected accents and bold graphics. Playful triangle tops, body-hugging mini dresses and slinky skirts are complemented by elevated textures and fabrications. Faux twists on feathers and fur lend a softness to edgy faux-leather and whimsical rhinestone embellishments.
